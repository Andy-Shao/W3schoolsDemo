The AND & OR Operators

The SQL AND & OR Operators

The AND operator displays a record if both the first condition AND the second condition are true.

The OR operator displays a record if either the first condition OR the second condition is true.

AND Operator Example

The following SQL statment selects all customers from the country "Germany" AND the city "Berlin", in the "Customers" table:

SELECT * FROM Customers WHERE Country='Germany' AND City='Berlin';

OR Operator Example

The following SQL statement selects all customers from the city "Berlin" OR "munchen", in the "Customers" table:

SELECT * FROM Customers WHERE City='Berlin' OR City='Munchen';
